What we do

Nougat Research

Original investigations into topics of public interest, primarily technology, media, and public affairs. All research is published openly, free to read and free to use. Code produced in the process is open source.

Nougat Media

Editorial content built on top of Nougat Research. Articles, written analyses, and visual explainers that make research findings accessible to a general audience.

Principles

01

Open by default

All research is published under open licenses. Code is open source. Nothing is paywalled.

02

Public interest first

Topics are chosen because they matter to people, not because they are convenient or popular.

03

Accessible writing

Findings are written for a general audience. No academic gatekeeping.
